What is the Haber Process?

The Haber Process, developed by Fritz Haber in 1909, is a method for synthesizing ammonia (NH₃) from nitrogen and hydrogen gases. This breakthrough enabled the mass production of nitrogen-based fertilizers, revolutionizing agriculture and dramatically increasing crop yields worldwide.
